Capabilities

One toolkit, every kind of voice

From a script that needs narrating to a voice that needs cloning, Voicecast covers the full range of voiceover work in a single workspace.

Text to speech

Paste a script, pick a voice, and generate natural narration in seconds. Adjust pacing, emphasis, and tone line by line.

Voice cloning

Upload a short sample and create a reusable digital voice that keeps your tone, pacing, and character across every project.

Speech to speech

Record yourself, then re-render the take in a different voice — your timing and emotion stay intact, only the voice changes.

Voice effects

Layer in room tone, radio filters, or pitch shaping to fit a scene — preview every effect instantly, no plugins required.

Dubbing & localization

Translate a video into 30+ languages while keeping each speaker's original voice — built for global content teams.

API access

Pipe voice generation straight into your own product or pipeline with a simple, well-documented REST API.
